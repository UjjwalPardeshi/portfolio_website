About Me

I’m Ujjwal Surajkumar Pardeshi, a machine learning engineer and researcher passionate about AI, deep learning, and computer vision. Currently, I am pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science (IoT) at SRM Institute of Science & Technology (2022–2026).

My work spans vision systems for reflection removal and depth estimation, smart campus solutions, and deepfake detection. I’ve interned at Samsung Research, led research at IEEE SRM, and received honors including the AIOT Expo Award. My goal is to deploy impactful AI-powered solutions and welcome new collaborations.
